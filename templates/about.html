About Us

Sri Veerabhadra Nursery is a renowned nursery specializing in the cultivation and distribution of a wide variety of plants. Established in 1995, we have been serving customers with dedication and commitment for over two decades.

Our nursery is spread over a vast area, allowing us to grow plants in optimal conditions. We use advanced cultivation techniques and adhere to strict quality standards to ensure that our plants are healthy and vibrant.

At Sri Veerabhadra Nursery, we offer a diverse range of plants, including trees, shrubs, flowers, ornamental grasses, and more. Whether you're a gardening enthusiast, landscaper, or developer, we have the perfect plants to meet your needs.

Our team consists of experienced professionals who are passionate about plants and landscaping. From our knowledgeable staff to our skilled landscapers and designers, everyone at Sri Veerabhadra Nursery is committed to providing exceptional service and support.

As a customer-centric company, we prioritize customer satisfaction above all else. We strive to exceed our customers' expectations by offering high-quality plants, personalized service, and expert advice.
